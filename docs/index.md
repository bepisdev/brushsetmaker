## What is BrushsetMaker?

BrushsetMaker is a specialized macOS application designed for digital artists and Procreate users who need to compile and organize brush collections efficiently. Whether you're creating brush packs for distribution or simply organizing your personal collection, BrushsetMaker automates the tedious process of packaging brushes into the proper format.

## Open Source & Transparent

BrushsetMaker is completely open source. Review the code, contribute improvements, or customize it for your specific needs. Built with modern Python tools and fully open sourced under the MIT license.
